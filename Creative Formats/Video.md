# Recommended Video Formats
## As at 12/20/2019
## Note: Framerate should be set at the video's native frame rate

### For YouTube
Container: MP4

Audio codec: AAC-LC

Video codec: H.264

#### Bitrate:

Video:

1080p - 8-12 Mbps
///
4k - 45-68 Mbps

Audio:
128 - 512 kbps

Aspect ratio: 16.9

### For Vimeo
Audio codec: AAC-LC

Video codec: H.264

#### Bitrate:

Video:

1080p - 10-20 Mbps
///
4k - 30-60 Mbps
///
8k - 50-80 Mbps

Audio:
320 kbps

Aspect ratio: 16.9
